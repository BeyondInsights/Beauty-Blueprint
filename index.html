CASE STUDY

From Viral Moments to Brand Momentum

Transforming IGNITER* to FIRE ICON*

THE CHALLENGE

Laneige attracts significant levels of passion among purchasers and consumers familiar with it, but needs to grow penetration. Despite viral success with Lip Sleeping Mask, the K-beauty brand faces Igniter* challenges: strong emotional pull among existing users but limited mainstream adoption.

Key Obstacles Include:

Hero Product Dependency

Revenue concentrated in a few viral products vs. portfolio breadth

Single-Purchase Pattern

High trial but low repeat purchasing beyond hero items

Gen Z K-Beauty Interest

Untapped younger demographic curious about Korean innovation

THE BEYOND INSIGHTS BLUEPRINT APPROACH

Brand Edge Assessment

Authentic Strengths Discovery

Revealed Laneige's authentic strengths, isolating genuine brand equity from media spend effects and size vs competitors. Analysis showed unexpected strength in relatability, but hidden weaknesses in empowering messages.

Consumer Segmentation

Strategic Target Identification

Revealed that current hero product buyers are overly represented by "Purpose-Driven Changemakers" Designer Trendsetters* and Cultural Visionaries*" totaling 68% of sales, an advantage over key competitors - presenting targeting advantage, including among Gen Z.

Infidelity Index

Consumer Relationship Mapping

Mapped consumer relationship stages, discovering 46% of familiar never purchased are "Curious*" while 40% of lapsed "Reconcilable*"—identifying how their perception of brands results in their purchase choices vs Laneige – and where Laneige can assert authority.

Loyalty Multiverse

Emotional Pathway Uncovering

Uncovered emotional pathways to drive growth of the "Curious*" and "Reconcilable*" to increase purchase consideration by reconciling messaging tensions and prioritizing marketing initiatives.

TRANSFORMATION STRATEGY

Our comprehensive Blueprint analysis revealed the precise pathway to transform Laneige from a viral product phenomenon into a lasting brand powerhouse. By understanding the emotional drivers and purchase barriers of key segments, we identified how to convert viral moments into sustainable momentum.
