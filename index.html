The Blueprint Discovery Process

1

Brand Edge Assessment

Finding the Hidden Truth

What we discovered:

Perceived strength in "innovation"
was actually table stakes in K-beauty

The real differentiator?
An unexpected authenticity in relatability that competitors overlooked

Gap:
Empowerment messaging wasn't landing despite being central to campaigns

The insight: Laneige was trying to be what they thought consumers wanted, not leveraging what made them genuinely different.
2

Consumer Segmentation

Beyond Demographics

The surprise finding:

Instead of the expected beauty enthusiasts, Laneige's core buyers were lifestyle curators:

Purpose-Driven Changemakers
28%

Seek brands that align with their values of sustainability and social impact. They curate a conscious lifestyle.

Designer Trendsetters
24%

Look for premium aesthetics and innovation. They curate for visual impact and staying ahead of trends.

Cultural Visionaries
16%

Want authentic cultural stories and heritage. They curate experiences that connect to deeper meanings.

Laneige opportunity: Position as the K-beauty brand that enables each segment's unique lifestyle curation - from conscious beauty rituals to trendsetting routines to authentic cultural experiences.
3

Infidelity Index

The Relationship Status

The opportunity map:

46%

of aware non-buyers are "Curious" - they know you, they're interested, but something's holding them back

40%

of lapsed buyers are "Reconcilable" - they tried you once but didn't return

What's blocking them:

  • Curious consumers: Don't see how Laneige fits their complete beauty routine beyond the hero mask
  • Reconcilable buyers: Loved the product but don't know what to try next in the line
The revelation: 86% of your growth potential already knows and likes you - they just need the right nudge to convert or return.
4

Loyalty Multiverse Mapping

The Emotional Journey

The conversion drivers by segment:

Curious → Customer Journey
  • Trigger: Show how hero product fits into complete K-beauty ritual
  • Message: "Your Lip Sleeping Mask is just the beginning of glass skin"
  • Tactic: Bundle recommendations based on skin concerns, not just products
Reconcilable → Repeat Journey
  • Trigger: Personalized "next step" recommendations after first purchase
  • Message: "Loved the mask? Your skin will love this next"
  • Tactic: Time-based follow-ups when they're likely running low
Competitor Conquest
  • Target: Heritage brand users seeking innovation without losing efficacy
  • Message: "K-beauty innovation meets the quality you trust"
  • Tactic: Side-by-side ingredient comparisons showing superior formulations
3

The Transformation Strategy

From Igniter to Fire Icon: A 3-Phase Approach

1

Reframe the Narrative

  • Stop selling products, start sharing a lifestyle
  • Lead with values that resonate with Changemakers and Visionaries
  • Use Brand Edge insights to emphasize authentic relatability
2

Build the Ecosystem

  • Introduce complementary products through the lens of the hero product
  • Create content that shows the full Korean beauty routine
  • Target Curious and Reconcilable segments with specific barrier-addressing messages
3

Sustain the Momentum

  • Launch loyalty program designed for lifestyle curators, not point collectors
  • Partner with purpose-driven initiatives that match segment values
  • Measure success through repeat purchase rate, not just acquisition
