When Viral Success Creates Hidden Vulnerability

Significant Revenue concentration
in one hero product
→

The Igniter Trap:

  • Viral momentum eventually slows
  • Portfolio remains undiscovered
  • Growth limited to single SKU

Laneige's Lip Sleeping Mask achieved remarkable viral success. But with significant revenue concentration in a single SKU, the brand faced a critical inflection point. Our challenge: transform fleeting viral curiosity into sustainable portfolio loyalty.

What Our Blueprint Analysis Revealed:

Brand Edge™

Discovering Authentic Differentiation

Our Brand Edge™ analysis strips away the noise of paid media and size advantages to reveal a brand's true competitive strengths. For Laneige, this deep-dive uncovered a surprising insight:

What Competitors Fight On:
  • Clinical efficacy claims
  • Ingredient percentages
  • Before/after visuals
Laneige's Untapped Territory:
  • "Bedtime ritual romance"
  • Self-care as daily luxury
  • K-beauty mystique meets accessibility

This emotional whitespace represented a defensible position that competitors couldn't easily replicate – and consumers were already associating with the brand, just not acting on it.

Infidelity Index™

Mapping the Complete Loyalty Journey

Our proprietary Infidelity Index™ goes beyond simple awareness metrics to map exactly where consumers sit in their relationship with your brand – and what moves them forward or backward. For Laneige, we discovered:

46% "Curious" Non-Buyers

Aware of the mask, love the concept, but haven't purchased

40% "Reconcilable" Lapsed

Bought once, satisfied, but never repurchased

The Critical Insight:

These segments weren't defecting to competitors. Our analysis revealed they simply didn't realize Laneige offered a complete skincare routine. The Lip Sleeping Mask's viral success had paradoxically limited brand perception to a single product.

Migration Triggers Identified:
  • For "Curious": Ingredient education + routine visualization
  • For "Reconcilable": Bundle value messaging + complementary product discovery
Loyalty Multiverse™

Traditional research tests messages in isolation. Our Loyalty Multiverse™ methodology tests combinations of functional and emotional attributes to find the optimal conversion formula. Through systematic testing of 47 attribute combinations, we identified:
